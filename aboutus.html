About AYF Calabar

We empower individuals to become devoted followers of Christ through training, education, discipleship, and mentorship. Our mission is to foster spiritual growth, maturity, and obedience to Christ's teachings, developing spiritual leaders who positively impact their communities.








Our Mission

To train and develop Youths in the doctrine of Christianity towards attainment of the standard required of a true follower of Christ by making themselves useful to the church, the Nation and the Commuunity, striving daily to emulate the ideals of the perfect Man (Jesus)
